Welcome to the website dedicated to Billie Eilish's concerts in Barcelona!


We've been organizing various fan projects for the community for some time now during these very special days. Among them is an app/website that will allow us to project a complete rainbow across the stands. To do your part, just go to the website for Android and iOS. Once there, wait for Billie to start singing Birds of a Feather to choose your sector and project a color onto your phone screen. Please set the brightness to maximum!
